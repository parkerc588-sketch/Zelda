The Legend Returns

Released in 2006, Twilight Princess took the Zelda series into a darker, more realistic direction. Set centuries after Ocarina of Time, players follow Link, a young farmhand from Ordon Village, who must prevent Hyrule from being engulfed by a corrupted parallel dimension known as the Twilight Realm.

Key Features

Wolf Link

When entering the Twilight, Link transforms into a divine beast. Use enhanced senses to track scents and fight alongside the mysterious Midna.

Hidden Skills

Learn advanced sword techniques like the Ending Blow and Helm Splitter from the ghostly Hero's Shade.

The Twilight Realm

Explore a world frozen in shadow, where you must restore light to the Provinces of Hyrule.

Classic Dungeons

Navigate some of the series' most acclaimed temples, from the Arbiter's Grounds to the Snowpeak Ruins.

Major Characters

  1. Link: The chosen hero bearing the Triforce of Courage.
  2. Midna: The Twilight Spark—a mischievous guide with a secret past.
  3. Zant: The Usurper King who brought shadow to the world of light.
  4. Princess Zelda: The sovereign of Hyrule who sacrificed her freedom to save her people.
